Simple Steak Recipe

Description

A delicious, mouth-watering steak, cooked to perfection. This recipe is a must know for any connoisseur on meat. No pun intended. It's quick. It's delicious. It's easy.

Ingredients

  • Grass Fed, 12-16oz Top Sirloin Steak
  • One diced Garlic Clove
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Tbsp of Butter or Butter Substitute
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Bring that steak out in the open and pat it dry with a paper towel.
  2. Now coat both sides with plenty of Salt and Pepper.
  3. Let the steak rest for about 10 minutes to bring it to room temp. This is to avoid undercooking the meat.
  4. Set a pan (Cast Iron Skillet works best!) on the stove at Medium Heat.
  5. Apply a nice thin layer of your Extra Virgin Olive Oil to the Pan. Deep enough to cover about 1/8th of the bottom of the steak. Don't add the steak just yet!
  6. Once the oil barely starts to smoke, throw that meat in there! (Carfully of course)
  7. Add your choice of Butter to the pan, allowing it to fully melt.
  8. Add the diced Garlic and use a ladle to cover the steak with butter and Garlic.
  9. After 5 minutes, flip steak and repeat the ladling process.
  10. After another 5 minutes, remove the steak and place it on a plate and allow it to rest for 5 minutes. This allows the muscle fibers to relax which results in a more tender steak.
  11. Dig in! Pair with your favorite Whiskey for bonus points!

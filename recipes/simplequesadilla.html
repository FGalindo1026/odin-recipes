Simple Quesadilla Recipe

Description

Do you like cheese? Who on earth doesn't? Sorry Lactose Intolerant peeps! Here's another simple recipe from ya boy. Pull out that pan and crank up the heat!

Ingredients

  • 1 block of Muenster Cheese. Resist temptations to use the whole block!
  • 2 Flour or Corn Tortillas
  • Butter or Butter Substitute
  • Sour Cream for Dip
  • Salsa for Dip
  • Hot Sauce of your choice

Steps

  1. First bring a properly sized pan on the stove to medium heat.
  2. While thats warming up, cut up enough slices of cheese to cover your tortilla.
  3. Once thats done, drip some hot sauce over the cheese then cover it up with the second tortilla.
  4. Now throw a spoonful of your choice of butter on the pan and allow it to melt. Once it's melted, spread it around the pan.
  5. Gently place the Quesadilla on the pan and cover said pan.
  6. After 3 Minutes, flip your quesadilla.
  7. After another 3 minutes slide her on a plate, line up those dips and go to town!
